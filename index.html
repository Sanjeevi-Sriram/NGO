About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla.

The organization works towards creating sustainable social impact by empowering communities through education, food distribution, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, youth leadership, skill development, and humanitarian initiatives.

The foundation believes that positive change begins when individuals come together to serve society with compassion, innovation, and dedication.

The organization is committed to improving lives while creating opportunities for people across India.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, compassion, innovation, environmental responsibility, and sustainable social development.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where every individual has equal opportunities to grow, learn, and lead a dignified life.
